Daily Eco Tips

Simple changes you can make today.

Turn Off the Tap

Save up to 6 liters of water per minute by turning off the tap while brushing your teeth.

Bring Your Own Bag

Reusable bags save hundreds of plastic bags from ending up in landfills and oceans every year.

Go Meatless Mondays

Skipping meat for just one day a week reduces your carbon footprint significantly.
